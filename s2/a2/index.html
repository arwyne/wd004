using the if-else statement, create a grading system where you have four scores that you need to get the average. supply the equivalent qpi and the student's status (pass or fail)

QPI System

  • if the average is not a number, log in the console 'not a number'. else, dispaly the average.
  • average score is from 0 up to less than 55 gets the student a 5.0 qpi and a failed status
  • average score is from 55 up to less than 60 gets the student a 4.0 qpi and a failed status
  • average score is from 60 up to less than 65 gets the student a 3.0 qpi and a passed status
  • average score is from 65 up to less than 70 gets the student a 2.75 qpi and a passed status
  • average score is from 70 up to less than 75 gets the student a 2.5 qpi and a passed status
  • average score is from 75 up to less than 80 gets the student a 2.25 qpi and a passed status
  • average score is from 80 up to less than 84 gets the student a 2 qpi and a passed status
  • average score is from 84 up to less than 87 gets the student a 1.75 qpi and a passed status
  • average score is from 87 up to less than 90 gets the student a 1.5 qpi and a passed status
  • average score is from 90 up to less than 93 gets the student a 1.25 qpi and a passed status
  • average score is from 93 up to less than 100 gets the student a 1 qpi and a passed status
  • anything lower than 0 or greater than 100 gives out of bounds result
